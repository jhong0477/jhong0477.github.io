Atlas of Human Cardiac Anatomy

The Visible Heart® Lab is a division of the University of Minnesota's Surgery department, co-founded by Dr. Paul A. Iaizzo in collaboration with Medtronic to conduct translational systems physiology research which ranges from cellular and tissue studies to organ and whole body investigations. I am developing this interface as part of an overhaul project for the Atlas of Human Cardiac Anatomy, which features a comprehensive database of catalogued heart profiles.
